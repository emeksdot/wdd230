GARDEN CITY CHAMBER OF COMMERCE

Overview

Port Harcourt, the capital of Rivers State in the oil-rich South of Nigeria has many nicknames, but the most popular has always been - The Garden City. It is home to many multinational oil exploration companies like Shell (Anglo/Dutch), Agip (Italian), TotalFina (French) and many other businesses of varying sizes.

Site Purpose

The Garden City Chamber of Commerce is an association setup to allow businesses in Port Harcourt to work together to achieve common organizational goals like developing and growing their business by providing networking, marketing and educational opportunities. It also helps members to have their voices heard by publishing articles and in events hosting.

Audience

Our target audience includes the multinational companies, the retailers, SMEs, and sole-business owners operating in Port Harcourt and its immediate environs.

Scenarios

  • How do I join the Garden City Chamber of Commerce?
  • Are the membership dues paid as lumpsum or installmentally?
  • What are the benefits of joining the chamber?
